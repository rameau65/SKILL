# BRAND_ENGINE

## Purpose
Turn a project, organization, or personal creative identity into a coherent brand system.

## Input
- Brand name
- Mission
- Audience
- Tone
- Visual direction
- Content goals

## Output
- Brand philosophy
- Slogan
- Tone and manner
- Visual identity direction
- Color strategy
- Content pillars
- Brand story

## Rule
A brand is a repeated emotional promise. Keep message, visual, voice, and distribution consistent.
