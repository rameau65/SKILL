# NOVEL_ENGINE

## Purpose
Expand ideas, worlds, and characters into novels, fairy tales, essays, or serialized stories.

## Input
- Idea
- Theme
- World Bible
- Characters
- Target audience
- Genre

## Output
- Logline
- Synopsis
- Chapter outline
- World bible expansion
- Character profiles
- Sample scene
- Full manuscript plan

## Workflow
Idea → Theme → Character → Conflict → World → Outline → Scene → Draft → Review

## Rule
Even philosophical or educational content should become readable through character, scene, and emotional movement.
