# MARKETING_ENGINE

## Purpose
Convert finished content or brand assets into distribution, promotion, and monetization materials.

## Input
- Product/content
- Audience
- Platform
- Goal
- Budget/time constraints

## Output
- Campaign concept
- Landing page copy
- Blog post
- SNS posts
- Shorts package
- Email copy
- Monetization route

## Rule
Marketing should not distort the work. It should make the value easier to find, understand, and share.
