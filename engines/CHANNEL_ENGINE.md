# CHANNEL_ENGINE

## Purpose
Design and operate a YouTube or social content channel as a repeatable creative system.

## Input
- Brand
- Audience
- Theme
- Upload frequency
- Monetization goal

## Output
- Channel concept
- Content pillars
- Series structure
- Thumbnail system
- Title formula
- Shorts strategy
- Long-form strategy
- Monetization roadmap
- Monthly content calendar

## Channel Pillars Example
1. Meditation cinema
2. AI art process
3. Short wisdom clips
4. Behind-the-scenes workflow
5. Music/soundscape releases

## Rule
A channel is not a pile of videos. It is a repeatable promise to the audience.
