# VIDEO_ENGINE

## Purpose
Convert still images, scripts, or concepts into motion/video prompts.

## Tools
Hailuo, Grok Imagine, Kling, Seedance, Runway, Sora.

## Output Table

| Cut | Mode | Length | Image/Scene | Camera | Subject Motion | Atmosphere | Prompt |
|---|---|---:|---|---|---|---|---|

## Image-to-Video Base Prompt
Animate this still image into a cinematic shot while preserving the original composition, identity, color palette, and style. Add subtle natural motion, atmospheric particles, gentle camera movement, and matching ambient mood. Avoid text artifacts, distorted hands, unstable faces, sudden cuts, and unwanted objects.

## Rule
Prefer subtle motion over large movement. Use 4–6 second cuts by default.
