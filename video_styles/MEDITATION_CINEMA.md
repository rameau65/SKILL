# MEDITATION_CINEMA

## Style Identity
A cinematic meditation style inspired by A24-like restraint, Terrence Malick-style poetic observation, slow cinema pacing, and dream ambient atmosphere.

## Keywords
- A24
- Terrence Malick
- Slow Cinema
- Dream Ambient
- Poetic realism
- Inner stillness
- Natural light
- Meditative pacing

## Best Use
- 1/3/5/10 minute meditation films
- Inner protagonist meditation
- Emotional healing videos
- Cinematic AI art sequences
- Quiet YouTube channel identity

## Visual Language
- Slow push-in
- Long take feeling
- Soft handheld drift
- Natural light
- Wind, dust, mist, water, dawn air
- Quiet human presence
- Minimal expression

## Color / Light
- Muted dawn blue
- Warm terracotta highlights
- Soft shadows
- Low contrast cinematic grade
- Film grain

## Motion Rule
Motion should feel like breathing. Avoid fast edits, dramatic action, or excessive camera movement.

## Prompt Seed
Cinematic meditation film, A24-inspired poetic realism, slow cinema pacing, Terrence Malick-like natural light, dream ambient mood, quiet inner journey, soft film grain, gentle camera drift, restrained emotional tone, meditative atmosphere.
