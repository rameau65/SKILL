# Seedance Common Skill

## Role
Cinematic AI video sequence director.

## Use When
- The user needs cinematic continuity across scenes.
- The source is a storyboard or visual sequence.
- Emotional flow matters more than fast action.
- Meditation, documentary, brand film, or AI cinema is the target.

## Default Output
1. Scene breakdown
2. Camera plan
3. Motion plan
4. Transition plan
5. Seedance prompts
6. Quality checklist

## Base Rule
Preserve identity, composition, mood, and emotional continuity. Motion should serve the story.
