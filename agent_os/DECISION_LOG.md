# DECISION_LOG

## Purpose
Record why the system chose a pipeline, agent, engine, style, or tool path.

## Decision Log Template

| Time | Decision | Options Considered | Selected | Reason | Confidence | Risk |
|---|---|---|---|---|---:|---|

## Required Decisions To Log

- Pipeline selection
- Style preset selection
- Main video engine selection
- Supporting engine selection
- Music direction
- Delivery format
- Any major change from reference material

## Rule
Creative decisions should be explainable, not accidental.
