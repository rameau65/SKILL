# QUALITY_GATES

## Purpose
Define required review checkpoints before delivery.

## Gate List

### 1. Intake Gate
- Is the goal clear?
- Is the output format known?
- Are references and constraints identified?

### 2. Pipeline Gate
- Was the correct pipeline selected?
- Are required agents and engines defined?

### 3. Research Gate
- Are factual claims grounded?
- Are current sources needed?
- Are unverified claims flagged?

### 4. Proposal Gate
- Are there differentiated concepts?
- Is the tool path honest?
- Is the sample direction clear?

### 5. Story Gate
- Is the structure coherent?
- Is the emotional arc clear?

### 6. Visual Gate
- Is the visual style consistent?
- Are prompts usable?

### 7. Video Gate
- Is Seedance the correct main engine?
- Are supporting engines justified?
- Is motion serving emotion?

### 8. Music Gate
- Does sound support the mood?
- Are no-vocal/no-drum constraints respected when needed?

### 9. Delivery Gate
- Are all requested deliverables present?
- Is the output reusable?

### 10. Archive Gate
- Are prompts, decisions, and versions saved?

## Rule
No final delivery without at least one QUALITY_REVIEWER pass.
