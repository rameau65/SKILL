# WORLD_BIBLE

The World Bible stores the philosophy, mythology, tone, symbols, and recurring narrative logic of each project.

## Universal Structure

1. Core Philosophy
2. Keywords
3. Symbols
4. Emotional Tone
5. Visual Motifs
6. Narrative Rules
7. Forbidden Directions
8. Expansion Ideas

## Layers

### Hanmaum Layer
For philosophy, education, meditation, and living wisdom.

### Mind Scenes Layer
For cinematic meditation, emotion-as-landscape, AI art, music, and YouTube series.

### Universal Creative Layer
For books, documentaries, exhibitions, games, lectures, and new genres.

## Rule
World Bible must guide every engine: story, visual, video, music, education, document, and channel output.
