# MIND_SCENES_LAYER

## Concept

Emotions become landscapes. Thoughts become stories. Meditation becomes cinema.

## Core Themes

- Inner protagonist
- Quiet awakening
- Emotional weather
- Memory as space
- Healing through observation
- Everyday life as a cinematic meditation

## Visual Tone

A24-like quiet cinema, Korean dawn mood, poetic realism, dream ambient atmosphere, soft film grain, restrained color, introspective pacing.

## Music Tone

Instrumental, no vocals, no drums, ambient, melodic, minor key, loop-friendly.

## Content Uses

- 1/3/5/10 minute meditation videos
- AI art series
- YouTube channel
- Short-form poetic narration
- Soundscape packages
- Poster and thumbnail systems
